# **6. How to Contribute a Paper**

Students and researchers may submit papers by:

- creating a new file under `/research/papers/`  
- following the Multiverse session context  
- using W‑operators and RTT alignment  
- including continuity diagrams when relevant  
- linking the paper to open questions  
- adding the paper to this index  

All papers must be open‑access and reproducible.
